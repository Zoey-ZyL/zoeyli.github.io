Publications

BDEC: Enhancing learning credibility via post-quantum digital credentials

Abstract: This paper introduces BDEC, a framework leveraging post-quantum digital credentials to enhance the credibility of learning outcomes in digital education environments. Accepted at the 18th International Conference on Provable and Practical Security (2024).

Blockchain for smart logistics: Enhancing identity security, bidding transparency, and goods tracking

Abstract: This study explores the application of blockchain technology in smart logistics, focusing on improving identity security, bidding transparency, and the tracking of goods. Accepted at ACM BSCI 2024.

A blockchain-based digital identity management system via decentralized anonymous credentials

Abstract: This paper presents a blockchain-based digital identity management system that uses decentralized anonymous credentials to enhance privacy and security. Accepted at ACM BSCI 2024.

Transfer of learning from Metaverse to blockchain for secondary students: Implementation and effectiveness evaluation

Abstract: This study evaluates the transfer of learning from Metaverse environments to blockchain concepts for secondary students, focusing on implementation and effectiveness. Enhancing blockchain adoption through tailored software engineering: An industrial-grounded study in education credentialing

Abstract: This industrial-grounded study investigates how tailored software engineering practices can facilitate the adoption of blockchain in education credentialing systems. Distributed Ledger Technology, Just Accepted (2023).

CVallet: A blockchain-oriented application development for education and recruitment

Abstract: CVallet introduces a novel blockchain-oriented application designed to streamline processes in education and recruitment, focusing on transparency and efficiency. In X. Yuan, G. Bai, C. Alcaraz, & S. Majumdar (Eds.), Network and System Security, NSS 2022, Lecture Notes in Computer Science, vol 13787. Springer (2022).

Blockchain-based solutions for education credentialing system: Comparison and implications for future development

Abstract: This paper compares various blockchain-based solutions for education credentialing systems, providing insights and implications for future development in this field. In 2022 IEEE International Conference on Blockchain (Blockchain), pp. 79-86. IEEE (2022).
